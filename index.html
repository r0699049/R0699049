Internship

During the last year of my studies at Thomas More I worked as an intern at E.Care for 3 months (March - May 2020). When I was looking for a company to do my internship I stumbled upon E.Care, a Software Development business that creates, sells and implements software that supports the medical care process in hospitals. E.Care has a great working software with many satisfied customers, but the UI and UX of the application is getting a bit outdated, it's currently completely made in Windows Forms and it is not mobile friendly. To change that, they wanted to make a switch to a mobile friendly web-based variant of the software made with the Angular framework. That's where I come in, my job was to analyse various modules of the existing software, prototype and finally develop them in an Agile way. Once a module or a component of a module was done we packaged them into an Angular library so it could be installed and used in various projects. Since E.care has many modules we weren't able to rework them all, but we did finish as much as we could do in 3 months. These modules and components are now ready to be looked at by someone who will style the new software in a unique way.
